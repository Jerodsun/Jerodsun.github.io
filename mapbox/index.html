Why MapBox?

12/29/2019

To the MapBox Team:

My name is Jerod and I’m a student at Georgetown University’s School of Foreign Service. Thanks for posting this internship opportunity!

I enjoy looking for patterns. From AIME problems to Jane Street puzzles, I use opportunities to put analytical concepts into practice.


*I can do some frontend programming.

Last summer, I interned at Measurabl, a SaaS startup that helps companies aggregate, report and act on sustainability data. While I primarily worked on the proprietary building scoring system with scikit-learn, I also designed a workflow to fuzzy match addresses, automated devops tasks and fixed bugs relating to the data pipeline. During the school year, I work on NLP and data engineering tasks supporting policy analysis at Georgetown’s Center for Security and Emerging Technology.

GIS & Machine Learning

In my spare time, I’m working on a project analyzing voting patterns in my home state. I’m currently building a Machine Learning model that assigns a unique score to each census block group based on expected/actual vote percentages by fitting to a gamma distribution.

Every year, precinct boundaries change. There are 58 counties in California, and each county documents these changes differently. With QGIS, I area-scaled the precinct-by-precinct votes and then merged with census data. When I’m done with my project, I’ll use Mapbox to present my findings with the world!

So, why MapBox? (why me?)

I’m fluent with data structures and algorithms in Python, and I’m eager to master anything that’s required.

I’m looking for a motivated, passioante team that creates value – ideally, on a product that can be reproduced in unlimited quantities.

To me, MapBox’s business plan has infinite potential. As a framework that allows developers to connect to non-GIS professionals, it expands the applications of GIS to a diverse group of academics, researchers and hobbyists.

MapBox’s core values resonate with me. Accurate data visualization for individuals and companies fosters innovation and is something I want to be a part of. For this summer, I believe an internship with MapBox is the right place to start.

Thanks for considering my application!
