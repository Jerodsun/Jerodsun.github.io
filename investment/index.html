Why the Investment Office?

My name is Jerod and I’m a freshman in the SFS majoring in Science, Technology and International Affairs. I’m excited to apply for this internship with the Investment Office to immerse myself in the field of finance, but more importantly, to learn and grow with a passionate team.

This summer, I interned in data science at Measurabl, a SaaS company that helps companies aggregate, report and act on sustainability data.


*I can do some frontend programming.

Measurabl’s clients include national REITs and S&P Global. Working with representatives of the industry led me to explore opportunities in finance.

I enjoy looking for patterns. From AIME problems to Jane Street puzzles, I like to put mathematics, especially statistical modelling, into practice. As the head coach of my school’s Middle School Mathcounts team, I led weekly drills on various topics to prepare for Mathcounts and Math Field Day, where we had a blast.

In the SFS, I ultimately see myself in a niche of public policy to shape how technology promotes human rights internationally. To that end, there are many different paths. But they all require certain skills – skills that can be developed in finance.

Diligent investing combines three skills valuable everywhere: a fundamental understanding of human nature, raw brainpower, and self-control. For policymakers to understand the broader dynamics of the economy, they must have exposure to the field. A healthy financial industry expands the pool of resources for all and provides for prosperity at home and abroad.

So why me?

Everything I do, I resolve to do well. At the Investment Office, I’ll be eager to learn, communicate, and make sure my work is carried out well. I have experience with selenium for automation and enjoy applications of Python for automating tasks. Here at Georgetown, I believe an internship with the Investment Office is the right place to start.

Last month, I implemented calcuations of Black-Scholes-Merton and Brownian Motion into an API. POST some sample data into Black-Scholes-Merton Call Pricing and watch the magic happen!
