Why Adobe?

12/29/2019

To the Adobe Team:

My name is Jerod and I’m a student at Georgetown University’s School of Foreign Service. Thanks for posting this internship opportunity!

I enjoy looking for patterns. From AIME problems to Jane Street puzzles, I use opportunities to put analytical concepts into practice.


*I can do some frontend programming.

Last summer, I interned at Measurabl, a SaaS startup that helps companies aggregate, report and act on sustainability data. While I primarily worked on the proprietary building scoring system with scikit-learn, I also created visualizations of our building property types for the sales team, automated devops tasks and fixed bugs relating to the data pipeline. During the school year, I work on NLP and data engineering tasks supporting policy analysis at Georgetown’s Center for Security and Emerging Technology.

Machine Learning

In my spare time, I’m working on a project analyzing voting patterns in my home state. I’m currently building a Machine Learning model that assigns a unique score to each census block group based on expected/actual vote percentages by fitting to a gamma distribution.

So, why Adobe? (why me?)

I’m fluent with data structures and algorithms in Python, and I’m eager to master anything that’s required.

I’m looking for a motivated, passioante team that creates value – ideally, on a product that can be reproduced in unlimited quantities.

To me, MapBox’s business plan has infinite potential. As a framework that allows developers to connect to non-GIS professionals, it expands the applications of GIS to a diverse group of academics, researchers and hobbyists.

Adobe’s core values resonate with me. Accurate data visualization for individuals and companies fosters innovation and is something I want to be a part of. For this summer, I believe an internship with Adobe is the right place to start.

Thanks for considering my application!
