Why ZenDesk?

To the ZenDesk Team:

My name is Jerod and I’m a student at Georgetown University’s School of Foreign Service. Thanks for posting this internship opportunity!

I enjoy looking for patterns. From AIME problems to Jane Street puzzles, I use opportunities to put analytical concepts into practice.

I have a data structures and algorithms background in Python. Last summer, I interned at Measurabl, a SaaS startup that helps companies aggregate, report and act on sustainability data. While I primarily worked on the proprietary building scoring system with scikit-learn, I also created visualizations of our building property types for the sales team, automated devops tasks and fixed bugs relating to the data pipeline. During the school year, I work on NLP and data engineering tasks supporting policy analysis at Georgetown’s Center for Security and Emerging Technology.

I’m currently working on a project analyzing voting patterns in my home state. How do demographic factors predict voting patterns? What kind of precincts contain the most split ticket voters? In order to answer these questions, I built a gradient boosting regression model to assign percentile rankings for the 2016 Senate Race by fitting on a gamma distribution to score each census block group.

Zendesk’s values and impact resonate with me. I’m looking for a motivated, passionate team that creates value – ideally, on a product that can be reproduced in unlimited quantities. Providing the digital resources to foster innovation is something I want to be a part of in a data science role. I believe an internship with ZenDesk is the right place to start.

Thanks for considering my application!

Jerod
